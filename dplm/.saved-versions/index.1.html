Distinguishable-particle lattice model (DPLM)

The following 2 videos show the dynamics of DPLM from kinetic Monte Carlo simulation at a high temperature (T = 0.5) and a low temperature (T = 0.16) respectively. Both system consists of 1592 particles and 8 voids in a 40 x 40 square lattice (phi_v = 0.005). Particles are randomly colored to indicate their distinguishable nature. In the first video (15fps), consecutive frames correspond to a time lapse of 10^-5. In the second video (30fps), consecutive frames correspond to a time lapse of 10. The simulations were visualized using OpenGL. Direct download links: highT.mp4 (3MB), lowT.mp4 (13MB).
